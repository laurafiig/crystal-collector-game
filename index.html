Instructions:

The goal of the game is to match your score to the goal displayed below. If your score is higher than the goal, you will lose.

When you click on one of the four crystals below it's value will be added to your total score.

Your goal will be assigned randomly at the beginning of the game. Each of the four crystals below will be assigned a random value at the beginning of the game.

The goal and crystal values will reset to a new value for each game.
